Base Jam was my first game development project. In the first semester of my Bachelor's first year, we were put into teams of 2 to recreate an Atari classic. Our team was given Space Invaders.

Resources

  • Game Maker Studio

Base Jam was a very ambitious project for a first timer. I was in charge of programming and design. I came up with the concept of batting back the projectiles from the aliens as I thought it would be more intense and fun to play. The player can dash from left to right, and swing their bat. After 150 points, the player becomes supercharged and their next hit will eliminate multiple aliens.

I programmed the player movement, the enemy movement, the controller functions, the win/loss conditions, and the highscore tables which compares all players stored on a local file.
